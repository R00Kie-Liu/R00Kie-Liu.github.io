<h2 style="margin: 60px 0px 10px;">Short Bio</h2>


Currently, Huabin Liu (刘华斌) is a researcher at the DeepLearning Lab of [Ant Research Institute](https://www.antresearch.com/), whose research focuses on **diffusion large language model (dLLM) pre-training**, with an emphasis on efficient training paradigms and MoE architectures.

I received my Ph.D. from Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU), working with [Prof. Weiyao Lin](https://weiyaolin.github.io/) on **video understanding and analysis**. From 2024 to 2025, I visited to the [VISLab](https://ivi.fnwi.uva.nl/vislab/) at University of Amsterdam as a visiting researcher for 1 year, advised by [Prof. Cees Snoek](https://www.ceessnoek.info/) and [Prof. Filip Ilievski](https://www.ilievski.info/). From 2023 to 2024, I was a research intern at the Shanghai AI Lab, working with [Xiao Sun](https://jimmysuen.github.io/).During 2019, I was lucky to be a research intern at Aibee, supervised by [Dian Li](https://scholar.google.com.hk/citations?user=rF7HU94AAAAJ) and [Jianguo Li](https://sites.google.com/site/leeplus/). Prior to this, I obtained my bachelor’s degree from [Xidian University](https://en.xidian.edu.cn/) in 2019.
